Streamlining Self-Service Upgrade and Licensing Management Lifecycle

Project Overview

Designing a seamless credit card experience for subscription upgrades (purchasing, billing, and licensing management) is vital in our PLG motion targeting SMB clients. By offering a frictionless upgrade journey, we aim to foster long-term customer relationships and empower SMB clients to maximize the potential of our product.

My Role

I have been fully committed to collaborating with the engineering team to design and deliver a seamless credit card experience for subscription upgrades across four modules. This entails creating a user-friendly journey that covers purchasing, billing, and licensing management aspects.

Time Period

6 Months

Impact

  • Implemented platform-wide purchase, billing, and license management.
  • Introduced license enforcement patterns across all modules.

Challenge

My design challenge is to establish a continuous lifecycle of licensing management from scratch, including Purchase/Upgrade to Paid Subscriptions, Manage Billing and Subscription, and Monitor Licensing Usage.

Strategy

Adopt the "One card, one billing admin, and identical billing date" strategy. This ensures that each card is used by only one billing admin and aligns yearly and monthly subscriptions to the same billing date. This streamlines billing and licensing management.

Solution

Create clear visual hierarchy and interactive price calculator to simplify the purchasing decision.
Establish “one card and identical billing cycle” design strategy for billing experience can reduce friction and complexity of multiple modules and plan subscription management.
Design well-structured licensing data visualization can provide insight on optimizing licensing strategies and lead to cost savings and a better-aligned licensing model for the team.
